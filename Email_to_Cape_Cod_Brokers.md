## 🎯 Customization Tips Before Sending

### **Personalize Each Email:**
- Mention something specific about their brokerage
- Reference their experience or specialty
- Note if you have mutual connections

### **Prepare for Responses:**
- Have your business plan ready to share
- Know your desired commission split (start with 70/30 in your favor)
- Be ready to explain your tech services in simple terms
- Prepare answers to common questions:
  - "How will you find clients?"
  - "What's your experience with property management?"
  - "How do you handle emergencies?"
  - "What insurance will you carry?"

### **Follow-Up Strategy:**
- Send initial email
- If no response in 5 days, send polite follow-up
- Consider calling after email if appropriate
- Track all communications in your spreadsheet

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### **Legal Considerations:**
- Do not misrepresent your qualifications
- Be clear this is a supervision arrangement, not employment
- Consult with attorney before signing any agreements
- Ensure proper insurance is in place

### **Commission Split Expectations:**
- **Typical ranges:** 50/50 to 80/20 (you get larger share)
- **Factors that improve your split:**
  - You bring your own clients
  - You handle all marketing/operations
  - You have relevant experience
  - You're offering unique tech services
- **Negotiation tip:** Start asking for 70/30, be willing to accept 60/40

### **What to Look for in a Broker:**
- Experience with property management (not just sales)
- Good reputation in Cape Cod community
- Reasonable commission structure
- Supportive of your business model
- Willing to provide guidance without micromanaging
