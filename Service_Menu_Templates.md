## 📝 Service Menu: FAQ Section (to include with menu)

### **Q: What's included in the setup fee?**
**A:** Installation of all included tech equipment, system configuration, testing, and owner training.

### **Q: Do you charge extra for maintenance calls?**
**A:** No - routine maintenance coordination is included in all packages. Emergency dispatch may have additional fees depending on package.

### **Q: Can I upgrade my package later?**
**A:** Yes! You can upgrade anytime. We'll prorate the difference and handle the tech upgrades.

### **Q: What smart home brands do you use?**
**A:** We prefer reliable, widely compatible brands: Nest, Ring, Philips Hue, Sonos, and others based on your needs.

### **Q: Do you handle Cape Cod's seasonal regulations?**
**A:** Yes - we manage MA short-term rental registration, local occupancy taxes, and ensure compliance with town regulations.

### **Q: What if I already have some smart home devices?**
**A:** We'll integrate your existing equipment whenever possible. Consultation includes assessment of current systems.

## 💰 Pricing Strategy & Justification

### **Commission Justification:**
- **Market Standard:** 20-35% for full-service management
- **Our Value Add:** Tech expertise included (competitors charge extra)
- **Transparency:** No hidden fees - setup fee covers all equipment/installation
- **Performance-Based:** Higher commissions for higher-value services

### **Setup Fee Rationale:**
- Covers equipment cost (we purchase at wholesale)
- Includes professional installation (2-4 hours labor)
- System configuration and testing
- Owner training and documentation
- **Note:** Equipment remains property owner's asset

### **Upsell Strategy:**
1. Start most clients at Essentials (lowest barrier)
2. Demonstrate value through performance
3. Recommend upgrades based on property needs
4. Use tiered pricing to make upgrades feel incremental
